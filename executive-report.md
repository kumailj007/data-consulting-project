# Executive Consulting Summary

## Business Challenge
The client faced declining growth and unclear performance across regions and product categories.

## Analytical Approach
Sales data was cleaned, modeled and analyzed using Python and SQL to extract meaningful business insights.

## Key Business Insights
- Electronics category generates the highest revenue
- North region outperforms other regions
- Customer retention opportunities identified among repeat buyers

## Strategic Recommendations
- Focus growth investments on high-performing regions and products
- Strengthen retention programs for valuable customers
- Improve inventory planning using predictive data analysis

## Expected Business Impact
- Increased revenue growth
- Higher customer lifetime value
- Improved operational efficiency
